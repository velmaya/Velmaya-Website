# Return / Exchange Policy

_Last updated: [DATE]_

We want you to love what you order. If something isn't right, here's how returns and exchanges work.

## Eligibility

- Returns/exchanges must be requested within [7] days of delivery.
- Items must be unworn, unwashed, with original tags attached, and in their original packaging.
- Items marked "Final Sale" (if any) are not eligible for return or exchange.

If a size doesn't fit, contact us via WhatsApp at +91 95970 75752 with your order number — we'll check stock availability for your size and arrange an exchange where possible.

## Returns for Refund

- Once we receive and inspect the returned item, refunds are processed to your original payment method via Razorpay within [5–7] business days.
- Return shipping costs are [borne by the customer / covered by Velmaya for defective items] — to be confirmed before launch.

## Damaged or Incorrect Items

If you receive a damaged, defective, or incorrect item, contact us within [48 hours] of delivery with photos via WhatsApp or shop.velmaya@gmail.com, and we'll arrange a replacement or refund at no extra cost to you.

## How to Start a Return/Exchange

1. Message us on WhatsApp at +91 95970 75752 or email shop.velmaya@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return.
2. We'll confirm eligibility and share return instructions.
3. Ship the item back as instructed; once received and inspected, we'll process your exchange or refund.

## Note on Sizing

Before requesting a size exchange, please check our size guide on each product page — it's there to help you choose right the first time and avoid the need for a return.
