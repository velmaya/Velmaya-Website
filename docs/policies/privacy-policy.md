# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: [DATE]_

Velmaya ("we", "us", "our") operates velmaya.com (the "Site"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding it.

## Information We Collect

- **Contact details**: name, email, phone number, shipping address — collected when you place an order, contact us via WhatsApp/Instagram, or join our newsletter.
- **Order information**: products purchased, order value, payment status (we do not store your card/UPI details — these are processed directly by Razorpay).
- **Checkout activity**: if you begin checkout but don't complete payment, we may retain the contact details and cart you entered so we can follow up about your order.
- **Usage data**: pages visited, device/browser type, approximate location — collected automatically via Google Analytics and Microsoft Clarity for site improvement purposes.

## How We Use Your Information

- To process and fulfil your orders, including shipping and customer support
- To respond to questions sent via WhatsApp, Instagram, or our contact form
- To send order confirmations and, where you've opted in, occasional updates about new collections
- To understand site usage and improve the shopping experience

## How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:
- **Razorpay**, our payment processor, to process payments securely
- **Shipping/logistics partners**, to deliver your order
- **Analytics providers** (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity), in aggregated/anonymized form where possible

## Data Storage

Order and account data is stored with Supabase (PostgreSQL hosting). Product and marketing images are stored with Cloudflare R2. Both providers maintain industry-standard security practices.

## Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at shop.velmaya@gmail.com or via WhatsApp at +91 95970 75752. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.

## Cookies

We use cookies for essential site functionality (cart persistence) and analytics (Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity). You can control cookie preferences via your browser settings.
